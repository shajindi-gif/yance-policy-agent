# Video Generation Prompts — YanCe Policy Agent

Visual generation prompts for 3 promotional videos (30s, 60s, 90s).
Designed for AI video tools (Runway Gen-3, Pika Labs, Kling, Sora) or traditional motion graphics production.

---

## Video 1: 30-Second Elevator Pitch

**Title:** "30秒了解衍策政策Agent"
**Duration:** 30 seconds
**Aspect Ratio:** 16:9 (1920 x 1080)
**Use Case:** WeChat video, LinkedIn post, website autoplay hero

### Scene Breakdown

| Time | Scene | Visual Description | Motion |
|------|-------|--------------------|--------|
| 0-3s | **Hook** | A messy desk with paper stacks and a cluttered Excel spreadsheet. Gray, muted colors. | Slow zoom in, slight camera shake to suggest frustration |
| 3-8s | **Transition** | The screen cracks/glitches and transforms into a clean digital dashboard. Colors shift from gray to navy/teal. | Fast zoom + glitch transition effect |
| 8-15s | **Demo** | Dashboard showing policy matching in action: company profile card appears, policies fly in and connect, scores animate from 0 to 92. | Smooth pan across dashboard UI, data animations |
| 15-22s | **Result** | A clean report generates on screen, sections appearing one by one. A green checkmark stamp appears on "推荐申请". | Sequential fade-in of report sections |
| 22-27s | **Traceability** | Three traceability chains extend from the recommendation: source document, date, region map pin. | Lines animate outward from center |
| 27-30s | **CTA** | Logo reveal with tagline: "衍策引擎 — 园区政策服务AI Agent" | Logo scales in, text fades in |

### Visual Generation Prompt (per scene)

**Scene 1 (Hook):**
```
A cluttered office desk photographed from above. Paper stacks, sticky notes, a computer
screen showing a messy Excel spreadsheet with hundreds of rows. Gray, muted color
grading. Slight camera shake. Frustrating, overwhelming mood. No people visible,
just hands reaching for papers at the edge of frame.
```

**Scene 2 (Transition):**
```
A computer screen displaying a dashboard that transforms from a messy gray spreadsheet
into a clean, modern digital interface. The transition happens as a digital glitch/wave
effect sweeps across the screen, changing colors from gray to deep navy blue and teal.
Clean, satisfying transformation. Motion graphics style.
```

**Scene 3 (Demo):**
```
A clean, modern dashboard UI animation. A company profile card slides in from the left.
Policy cards fly in from the right and connect to the company card with animated teal
lines. Score numbers animate from 0 to 92 with a satisfying counter effect. Green and
yellow status badges appear. Smooth, professional motion graphics. Navy and teal color
scheme on white background.
```

**Scene 4 (Result):**
```
A structured Markdown-style report appearing section by section on a clean white screen.
Each section fades in with a slight upward motion. A large green circular checkmark stamp
animates onto the "推荐申请" recommendation. Professional document generation animation.
```

**Scene 5 (Traceability):**
```
A central recommendation card in the middle of screen. Three animated chains extend
outward: one to a government document seal (labeled 可追溯), one to a calendar icon
(labeled 可解释), one to a map location pin (labeled 可复核). Lines animate with
flowing dot particles. Navy and teal on white background.
```

**Scene 6 (CTA):**
```
Logo reveal: the YanCe logo (abstract neural network forming a park gate) scales up
from center. Below it, the text "衍策引擎" fades in, followed by the tagline
"园区政策服务AI Agent". Clean navy blue background with subtle particle effects.
```

## Video 2: 60-Second Product Walkthrough

**Title:** "衍策政策Agent产品演示"
**Duration:** 60 seconds
**Aspect Ratio:** 16:9 (1920 x 1080)
**Use Case:** Product page, investor demo, conference booth loop

### Scene Breakdown

| Time | Scene | Visual | Motion |
|------|-------|--------|--------|
| 0-5s | **Problem** | Montage: park manager drowning in paper, Excel tabs, phone calls, missed deadlines | Quick cuts, handheld feel |
| 5-10s | **Pain Point** | Close-up of a policy document with key requirements highlighted but a company profile that doesn't match | Slow push-in, red highlights |
| 10-15s | **Solution Intro** | YanCe logo + "输入企业信息，AI匹配政策" text on clean background | Smooth logo animation |
| 15-25s | **Input Demo** | Animated form: company name, industry, stage, region, employees fill in. Data flows into the matching engine. | Sequential form animation |
| 25-35s | **Matching Engine** | Abstract visualization: company node in center, policy nodes around it, scoring lines connecting, scores appearing | Radial layout, animated connections |
| 35-45s | **Output** | Report sections generate: policy table, gap analysis checklist, risk assessment, service tickets | Sequential section reveals |
| 45-52s | **Traceability** | Click on a recommendation → trace back to source document, date, authority, region | Interactive drill-down animation |
| 52-57s | **Impact** | Split screen: before (chaos) vs after (organized dashboard). Stats overlay: "匹配效率提升80%" | Side-by-side comparison |
| 57-60s | **CTA** | Logo + "从徐汇开始，验证政策Agent的园区场景" | Clean logo reveal |

### Visual Generation Prompts (key scenes)

**Scene: Matching Engine Visualization (25-35s):**
```
An abstract data visualization animation. A central circular node labeled "企业" glows
in teal. Around it, 6 policy nodes arranged in a circle, each with a small document icon.
Animated scoring lines connect the company to each policy, with score numbers appearing
along the lines (92, 88, 85, 80, 72, 68). Higher-score connections glow brighter.
The visualization rotates slowly. Deep navy background with particle effects. Modern
data visualization style, smooth 60fps animation.
```

**Scene: Impact Split Screen (52-57s):**
```
A split-screen comparison. Left side: a gray, chaotic desk scene with papers, multiple
Excel windows, and tangled lines. Right side: a clean, modern dashboard with organized
cards, clear workflow, and a satisfaction indicator. An animated counter shows "匹配效率
提升80%" appearing as an overlay. The right side gradually expands to take over the left.
Professional motion graphics. Navy and teal color scheme.
```

## Video 3: 90-Second Brand Story

**Title:** "从徐汇出发 — 衍策政策Agent的品牌故事"
**Duration:** 90 seconds
**Aspect Ratio:** 16:9 (1920 x 1080)
**Use Case:** Brand film, investor pitch, conference keynote opening

### Scene Breakdown

| Time | Scene | Visual | Motion |
|------|-------|--------|--------|
| 0-10s | **Setting** | Aerial shot of Shanghai Xuhui district, modern buildings, tree-lined streets, tech park campus | Cinematic drone shot, slow pan |
| 10-18s | **Problem Statement** | Inside a park management office: stacks of policy documents, a frustrated manager (silhouette), phone ringing | Interior shot, warm but tense mood |
| 18-25s | **Insight** | "Every company in this park deserves to know what policies can help them." Text appears over the park skyline. | Slow zoom on skyline, text fade-in |
| 25-35s | **Solution** | The YanCe platform interface: clean input form, matching animation, report generation | Screen capture style, clean UI |
| 35-48s | **Key Features** | Three feature vignettes: (1) scoring algorithm, (2) gap analysis checklist, (3) service ticket generation | Split into three panels sequentially |
| 48-58s | **Traceability** | Deep dive: click on a policy match, trace it back through the chain to government source document | Interactive drill-down, satisfying |
| 58-68s | **Human in the Loop** | Park service staff reviewing the report, confirming recommendations, contacting company | Warm, collaborative scene (silhouettes) |
| 68-78s | **Vision** | Map of China with Xuhui glowing, then more cities lighting up one by one | Map animation, expanding network |
| 78-85s | **Impact Stats** | "6项政策匹配 / 92分最高匹配度 / 4张服务工单 / 100%可追溯" stats animate on screen | Clean counter animations |
| 85-90s | **CTA** | Logo + "从徐汇开始，验证政策Agent的园区场景" + contact info | Clean reveal, hold |

### Visual Generation Prompts (key scenes)

**Scene: Aerial Xuhui (0-10s):**
```
A cinematic aerial drone shot of Shanghai's Xuhui district at golden hour. Modern glass
office buildings, tree-lined boulevards, a clean industrial park campus with green
courtyards. The camera slowly pans and descends toward the park. Warm, hopeful lighting.
Photorealistic, shallow depth of field, cinematic color grading with subtle teal tones
in the shadows. Professional quality, 4K resolution.
```

**Scene: Vision Map (68-78s):**
```
A stylized map of China rendered in dark navy blue. Xuhui, Shanghai glows brightly in
teal. One by one, other cities begin to glow: Beijing, Shenzhen, Hangzhou, Chengdu,
Guangzhou, Wuhan, Nanjing. Connection lines form between cities creating a network.
The map has a subtle 3D elevation effect. Particles float upward from each glowing city.
Deep navy background, modern data visualization aesthetic. Smooth animation.
```

**Scene: Impact Stats (78-85s):**
```
Four large animated counters on a clean white background, arranged in a row. Each counter
shows a large number with a label below: "6项 政策匹配", "92分 最高匹配度", "4张 服务工单",
"100% 可追溯". Numbers animate from 0 to their final values with a satisfying ease-out
curve. Small icons above each number: document, star, ticket, shield. Navy and teal color
scheme. Clean, professional motion graphics.
```

## Production Notes

- **Frame Rate:** 30fps minimum, 60fps for data visualization scenes
- **Color Grade:** Consistent navy/teal brand palette across all videos
- **Text Overlays:** Add in post-production (After Effects / DaVinci Resolve)
- **Music:** See music-prompts.md for background music specifications
- **Accessibility:** Add Chinese subtitles for all spoken/narrated content
- **Export Settings:** H.264, 1080p, 8Mbps minimum bitrate
- **Vertical Versions:** Create 9:16 crops for Douyin/WeChat Moments
