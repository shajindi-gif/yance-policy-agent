# Logo Design Prompts — YanCe Policy Agent

Prompts for AI image generation (Midjourney, DALL-E, Stable Diffusion).
Focus: clean, professional, park-friendly, policy/AI themed.
Exclusions: no rockets, no stock charts, no robots, no weapons.

---

## Logo Prompt 1 — Abstract Neural Network + Park Gate

**Concept:** An abstract neural network pattern forming the silhouette of a traditional Chinese park gate (园区大门), symbolizing the bridge between AI intelligence and industrial park services.

**Prompt:**
```
A minimalist logo design on a white background. An abstract neural network pattern with
soft blue and teal nodes connected by thin gradient lines, forming the silhouette of a
modern Chinese industrial park entrance gate. Clean geometric shapes, professional
corporate style. No text. Flat design, vector aesthetic. Color palette: deep navy blue
(#1B3A5C), teal (#2CB5A0), soft white. Subtle gradient from blue to teal. Symmetrical
composition. No robots, no charts, no rockets.
```

**Style:** Flat vector, corporate identity
**Use case:** Primary logo, favicon, app icon

---

## Logo Prompt 2 — Policy Document + Data Flow

**Concept:** A stylized policy document (scroll/page) with flowing data streams emerging from it, representing the transformation from policy text to actionable intelligence.

**Prompt:**
```
A clean, modern logo on white background. A stylized document or scroll icon in deep
blue, with three flowing curved lines emerging from it in teal and green gradients,
representing data streams or workflow paths. Small circular dots along the streams
suggest processing nodes. Minimalist, professional, corporate identity style. No text.
Flat design. Colors: navy (#1B3A5C), teal (#2CB5A0), emerald green (#34D399).
No robots, no financial charts, no coins.
```

**Style:** Flat vector, minimal
**Use case:** Secondary logo, report header, document watermark

---

## Logo Prompt 3 — Building Blocks + Connection Map

**Concept:** Interconnected building blocks forming a simplified park/campus layout, with connection lines showing the relationship between enterprises, policies, and services.

**Prompt:**
```
A minimalist logo on white background. Five to six small geometric building shapes
(rectangles and squares) arranged in a campus/park layout, connected by subtle dotted
lines forming a network graph. Buildings in navy blue, connections in teal. One building
is highlighted in a brighter teal color representing the focal point. Clean, professional,
flat design. No text. Corporate identity style. Colors: navy (#1B3A5C), teal (#2CB5A0),
light gray (#E5E7EB). No people, no robots, no financial symbols.
```

**Style:** Isometric flat design
**Use case:** Presentation slides, park signage, partner badges

---

## Logo Prompt 4 — Compass + Checkmark

**Concept:** A compass rose combined with a checkmark, symbolizing guided policy navigation and verified recommendations.

**Prompt:**
```
A professional logo on white background. A modern compass rose with four cardinal
points, simplified to clean geometric lines. The north pointer is replaced by a
checkmark shape, blending navigation and verification symbolism. Compass in deep navy
blue, checkmark in vibrant teal. Circular border in light gray. Minimalist, corporate,
flat vector design. No text. Colors: navy (#1B3A5C), teal (#2CB5A0), light gray
(#E5E7EB). No maps, no globes, no financial icons.
```

**Style:** Circular badge, flat vector
**Use case:** Certification seal, verified recommendation stamp, email signature

---

## Logo Prompt 5 — Layered Hexagons

**Concept:** Three overlapping hexagons representing the three layers of policy matching: company profiling, policy matching, and service workflow generation.

**Prompt:**
```
A modern logo on white background. Three overlapping hexagonal shapes arranged in a
triangular pattern, each a different shade: navy blue, teal, and soft green. The
overlapping areas create blended color regions suggesting integration and synergy.
Small dots at the vertices of each hexagon suggest data points. Clean, professional,
corporate identity. Flat design, vector aesthetic. No text. Colors: navy (#1B3A5C),
teal (#2CB5A0), emerald (#34D399). No 3D effects, no gradients beyond the overlap
regions. No robots, no financial symbols.
```

**Style:** Geometric, flat vector
**Use case:** Brand pattern, background motif, social media avatar

---

## Usage Notes

- All prompts are designed for 1024x1024 square output
- Export final logos as SVG for scalability
- Recommended minimum size: 32x32 px (favicon)
- Test on both light and dark backgrounds
- Ensure the logo works in monochrome (single color) for print applications
