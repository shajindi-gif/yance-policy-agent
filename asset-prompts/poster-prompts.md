## Poster 1: 园区公司的政策服务Agent

### Design Brief

| Field | Content |
|-------|---------|
| **Title (CN)** | 园区公司的政策服务Agent |
| **Subtitle (CN)** | 一键匹配政策，智能生成服务工单 |
| **Tagline (EN)** | Your Park's AI Policy Copilot |
| **Size** | 1080 x 1920 px (9:16, vertical) |
| **Use Case** | WeChat Moments ad, trade show banner, pitch deck cover |

### Visual Description
A modern, clean poster with a gradient background (deep navy to teal). Central visual shows a stylized park/campus aerial view with small building icons, overlaid with flowing data streams connecting to policy document icons. The title sits at the top in large bold Chinese characters. Bottom area features three small feature icons: a checklist, a workflow diagram, and a shield (for traceability).

### Image Generation Prompt (Midjourney / DALL-E)
```
A professional vertical poster design, 9:16 aspect ratio. Background: smooth gradient
from deep navy blue (#1B3A5C) at top to teal (#2CB5A0) at bottom. Central illustration:
a bird's-eye view of a modern Chinese industrial park with 5-6 clean geometric buildings,
connected by glowing teal data flow lines to floating document icons. Style: flat vector
illustration, modern tech aesthetic, professional corporate. Top area left blank for text
overlay. Bottom area has three small circular icons: a checklist, a workflow arrow, and a
shield. Soft ambient lighting, no people, no robots, no stock charts. Clean, minimal,
professional Chinese tech company aesthetic.
--ar 9:16 --style raw --v 6
```

A horizontal poster emphasizing transparency. The left side shows a policy recommendation card with a score and recommendation text. ## General Design Guidelines

- **Primary Colors:** Navy (#1B3A5C), Teal (#2CB5A0), White (#FFFFFF)
- **Secondary Colors:** Emerald (#34D399), Light Gray (#F3F4F6), Dark Gray (#374151)
- **Typography:** Chinese: PingFang SC / Source Han Sans; English: Inter / SF Pro
- **Tone:** Professional, trustworthy, modern tech, government-friendly
- **Exclusions:** No rockets, no stock charts, no robots, no weapons, no gambling imagery
